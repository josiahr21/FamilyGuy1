Data Analysis with AntConc

For our Data Analysis, we wanted to see how often certain characters appeared throughout the show. We first started with the main character, Peter Griffin.

We wanted to view the most common phrases with Peter's name. We found it was best to use three worded phrases as two worded phrases were mostly the same. With four and five worded phrases, they became less common which was not useful in our findings
