Home Page

Our data collection is from season 21 and 22 of Family Guy. As we created our project, season 22 continues to release episodes. We stopped adding to our database at the end of March with epsiode 13, Lifeguard Meg.

Our inspiration for this project stems from the popular music website known as Genius. They are a music encyclopedia page that hosts thousonds upon thousands of song transcripts. One thing that makes them unique is they offer pop-ups on certain lyrics o provide some context. On these pop-ups, fans can comment their own opinion on what they believe the lyric means.

Project Info

Our project's purpose was to conduct a complete analysis and comprehension of Family Guy season 21 and 22 episode 13 by getting text files from each season's episodes and using them to create graphs to assist us comprehend it better.
